# Vegan Pesto

## Ingredients

* 6 oz fresh basil
* 1 cup pine nuts
* 2+ oz olive oil
* 5-10 cloves garlic
* Â½ cup nutritional yeast
* Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the basil, pine nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, and salt in a food processor.

2. Pulse until coarsely chopped and combined.

3. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil. Keep adding oil to your desired consistency, typically between 2 and 4 oz.

4. Taste and adjust salt as needed.
